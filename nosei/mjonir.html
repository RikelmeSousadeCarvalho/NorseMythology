Mjonir

It is probably the best known weapon in all of Norse Mythology. Belonging to the Norse god of strength and thunder, Thor, Mjolnir is an incredibly powerful warhammer capable of shattering entire mountains and summoning raging storms. It has a relatively short handle due to a ruse by Loki, who wanted to play a trick on Thor. This did not prevent him from magnificently using this powerful weapon.
